Born in Cairo 17 November 1938,
In  December 1949,the family emigrated to Israel  where  they lived in a new agricultural  settlement  called "Beit-Zait", It is a splendid place , in the heart  of the beautiful mountains surrounding Jerusalem.
After his military sevice in the air forces, he moved with his family to the city of Holon.
On 14 May 1963, married to Florette Betito,...
He began to work in El-Al, the Israely airline company, in Lod airport.
After some years , he was drafted to Teheran, then Istamboul, ,,Paris ...
Actually, living in Paris, he is general manager of El-Al  for Europe and South America.
Itshak Cohen , and Florette divorced.
