Haim (Vita) Rabie , son of Eliahou Nissim Rabiyaa and Farha is born in Iraq, in 1890.
At the age of 20, he leaves Iraq, immigrates in Egypt, and lives in the town of Tantah.
Married to Helene Mizrahi 17-07-1917,… the family lives in Tantah, where 9 children are born.
In 1943, the family moves and lives in Cairo.
In 1949, the family leaves Egypt, immigrates in Israel and lives in Ramleh.
He died in Israel on the 18-04-1958.
