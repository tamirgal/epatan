ENGLISH: Hayim Abada is born in Cairo, April 1917. Few details and info are known about his youth,.. He was 6 years old at the death of his father Daoud Abada in 1923.
In 1949, aged 32, he emigrated to Israel, alone, and lived in "Tirat Hacarmel",  a small town south of Haifa. On June 26, 1950, married to Esther Attiya, and have 8 children. Later on, in 1957, he brings in Israel, his mother Regina who stayed in Egypt with her daughters. He died in 1968, aged 51 years.
FRENCH: Hayim Abada, est ne au Caire en Avril 1917. Tres peu d'informations sur sa jeunesse en Egypte, sinon qu'il avait 6 ans lors du deces de son pere en 1923.
En 1949,  a l'age de 32 ans,il emigra en Israel, et s'installe a Tirat Hacarmel,  petite ville tout pres de Haifa.  Le 26 juin 1950, il se marie  avec Esther Attiya, et ont 8 enfants. Plus tard, en 1957,  il amene  en Israel, sa maman Regina qui etait restee en Egypte, pres de ses filles. Il est mort en 1968, a l'age de 51 ans, et enterre a Tirat Hacarmel.
