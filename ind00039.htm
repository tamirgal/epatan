Itshak Sasson

Born: 1863
Died: 2-Jul-1937    

Comments: Born in Bagdad, emigrated to Egypt in 1881 with parents,brothers,sisters. Married  in Cairo (Mar1885) to Marietta (Miriam) Attar.They lived in Louxor, then in Cairo,.Only 5 children survived  to advanced age, but many died very young. He died in Cairo at his daughter's  (Esther) appartment, 2 July 1937, aged 74 years old.
