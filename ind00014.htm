Victoria  Sasson Patan

Born: 4-Feb-1901
Died: 25-Oct-1983   Emigrated to Israel in 1957,with her 3 children, and 3 grandchildren.

Comments:
Victoria Patan, Born in Louxor the same day when news came, announcing the death of queen Victoria. So my grandma said: A queen is dead, and my princess is born. And they called her Victoria. Married to Ezra Patan in Cairo (Sept 1927) ... Widow in 1938...
In 1957 immigrated to Israel with her 3 children and 3 grandchildren.
She died in Israel, October 1983, aged 82.  (Elie Patan).

Victoria 's Family
