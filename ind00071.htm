Emilie Cohen Sasson

Born: 24-Aug-1916

Comments:
EMILIE COHEN SASSON: born in Cairo, the 24th of August 1916… Married to Albert Sasson, in Cairo on the 28th of February 1937. In 1982, she left Egypt with her husband, and immigrated to Israel, and lived in the city of Holon.
After the death of her husband Albert in January 1993, she lived in a home for aged people in the city of "Holon", untill she died on 2 May 2004
