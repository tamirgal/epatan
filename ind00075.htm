Mireille Cohen

Born: 25-Jul-1936
Died: 1955    

Comments: Born in Cairo (Egypt), 25 July 1936.
In December 1949,  the family emigrated to Israel  where  they lived in a new agricultural  settlement  called "Beit-Zait", It is a splendid place , in the heart  of the beautiful mountains surrounding Jerusalem.
Some years later, Mireille Cohen died after a long and painful sickness, aged 19.

Mireille's Heritage

Parents: Maurice (Moshe)  Cohen, Esther Sasson Cohen
Siblings: David Cohen, Rosette (Rosy) Cohen Hanan, Itshak (Zako) Cohen, Vicky Cohen Itshaki
