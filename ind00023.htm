Nissim Ravia

Born: 11-Feb-1932

Comments:
NISSIM RAVIA, born in Cairo, the 11th of February 1932… Immigrated in Israel, with his family in 1949. After his military service, graduated from the Israeli University in Tel-Aviv,(Economics), worked some years in important auditor offices and then opened his own private auditing office
Married to Miriam Patan in Tel-Aviv, on 27th September 1959…
